Thalipith Bhajani

MRP: ₹55, 250gm/Pkt.

Maharashtrian roasted multi-grain bread. Tastes best when consumed with curd or makkhan.

Thalipith (Crispy onion bread)

 4-5 Medium Pieces 30-40mins

Ingredients Quantity
Haveeka Thalipith Bhajani
 250 gm
Medium Onions, diced 2
Boiled Mashed Potato (optional) 1
Coriander, fine chop 1/2 cup
Fenugreek leaves, (optional) 1/4 cup
Paste of ginger, garlic & green chilli 2 Tbsp
Turmeric 1 Tsp
Red Chilli powder 1 Tbsp
Water, Oil and Butter / Makkhan

Let's Begin

  1. In a kneading bowl, add the above ingredients to bhajani & mix properly.
  2. Slowly add water to knead a dough, it should be soft enough to press with fingers.
  3. Take one ball of dough as per size you require, apply little water on plastic sheet & press the dough evenly with fingers to give it a flat disk shape.
  4. Put some oil in a fry/non-stick pan and shallow fry Thalipith from both the sides.

That's it ! Crispy Thalipith is ready, top it up with makhhan and dig in !

Amboli Flour

MRP: ₹40, 250gm/Pkt.

Amboli is versatile, easy and quick pancake with a crisp Maharashtrian touch to its flavour

Amboli

 15-20 Medium Pieces 30-40mins

Ingredients (Plain Amboli) Quantity
Haveeka Amboli Flour
 250 gm
Warm Water 1.5-2 Cups
Salt (per taste) 1-2 Tsp
Curd / Buttermilk 3 Tbsp
Add On (Masala Amboli) Quantity
Turmeric powder
 1 Tsp
Red Chilli Powder 1 Tbsp
Ginger Garlic Paste 1 Tbsp
Chopped vegetables of choice 1-2

Let's Begin

  1. In a kneading bowl, add the above ingredients to flour & mix properly (except vegetables).
  2. Slowly add warm water as you stir continuously to make a thick enough, pouring consistency batter. 
  3. Remove any lumps by stiring, then add finely chopped vegetables (optional).
  4. Keep the batter aside for 20 min.
  5. Pour the batter on a non-stick pan and spread evenly. For normal pan, add little oil or butter to grease the pan before pouring the batter. 

That's it ! Soft and spongy Amboli is ready, serve with your favourite chutney
