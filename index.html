Chakali Bhajani

500gm/Pkt.

A delicious, crunchy, spiky-textured spiral snack that is a festive specialty

Chakli (murukku)

 ~45 Pieces 30-40mins

Ingredients Quantity
Haveeka Chakli Bhajani
 500 gm
Chilli Powder 1.5 tbsp
Turmeric Powder 1/2 tbsp
Ajwain
 1 tbsp
Sesame seed ( Til)
 2 tbsp
Salt (as per taste)
 1.5 tbsp
Water
 350 ml
Oil
 30 ml

Let's Begin

  1. Take 350 ml of water, add all the ingredients except the Bhajani
  2. Heat the mixture to a boil
  3. Add Haveeka Chakli bhajani to this and mix properly
  4. Turn off the gas and let it sit for atleast 20 mins
  5. Add water as required to break the lumps and form the dough
  6. Use a "chakli press tool" to press out the dough in spiral shape
  7. Deep fry the spiral chakli using frying oil of your choice on high to medium flame

That's it ! Crunchy Chakli is ready !

Thalipith Bhajani

250gm/Pkt.

Maharashtrian roasted multi-grain bread. Tastes best when consumed with curd or makkhan.

Thalipith (Crispy onion bread)

 4-5 Medium Pieces 30-40mins

Ingredients Quantity
Haveeka Thalipith Bhajani
 250 gm
Medium Onions, diced 2
Boiled Mashed Potato (optional) 1
Coriander, fine chop 1/2 cup
Fenugreek leaves, (optional) 1/4 cup
Paste of ginger, garlic & green chilli 2 Tbsp
Turmeric 1 Tsp
Red Chilli powder 1 Tbsp
Water, Oil and Butter / Makkhan

Let's Begin

  1. In a kneading bowl, add the above ingredients to bhajani & mix properly.
  2. Slowly add water to knead a dough, it should be soft enough to press with fingers.
  3. Take one ball of dough as per size you require, apply little water on plastic sheet & press the dough evenly with fingers to give it a flat disk shape.
  4. Put some oil in a fry/non-stick pan and shallow fry Thalipith from both the sides.

That's it ! Crispy Thalipith is ready, top it up with makhhan and dig in !

Kombdi Vade

250gm/Pkt.

Native Konkani style bread that tastes delicious with chicken curry

Kombdivade / Malvani Vada

 15-20 Small Pieces 30-40mins

Ingredients Quantity
Haveeka Kombdi Vade Pith
 250 gm
Green Chilli Paste
 1 Tbsp
Salt (as per taste)
 1.5 Tbsp
Frying Oil

Let's Begin

  1. Add chilli paste to the Komdi vade mix and bind all ingredients with luke warm water until you form a dough
  2. Set the dough aside for atleast 30 min
  3. Take small ball size portions and press them flat to form a disc shape
  4. Deep fry the vada in frying oil and watch them puff.

That's it ! Kombdivade are ready, serve them with your favourite chicken/mutton/vegetable curry !

Upwas Bhajani

250gm/Pkt.

Gluten-free bread that is made to keep your will strong during the fasting

Upwas Bhajani

 5-6 Small Pieces 30-40mins

Ingredients Quantity
Haveeka Upwas Bhajani
 250 gm
Ginger Paste+Chilli Paste
 1.5 Tbsp
Boiled & Mashed Potato 2
Crushed groundnut 1/2 Cup
Salt (as per taste) 1.5 Tbsp

Let's Begin

  1. Add ginger and chilli paste, crushed groundnut and mashed potato to Upwas Bhajani mix
  2. Mix this mixture with minimum water till it become like a dough of Bhakari
  3. Take a small quantity of this mixture and spread it on a plastic sheet. Press with your fingers to flatten it like a Bhakari
  4. Shallow fry in ghee or oil both the sides until cooked

That's it ! Upwas Thalipith are ready, serve them with curd !

Amboli Flour

250gm/Pkt.

Amboli is versatile, easy and quick pancake with a crisp Maharashtrian touch to its flavour

Amboli

 15-20 Medium Pieces 30-40mins

Ingredients (Plain Amboli) Quantity
Haveeka Amboli Flour
 250 gm
Warm Water 1.5-2 Cups
Salt (per taste) 1-2 Tsp
Curd / Buttermilk 3 Tbsp
Add On (Masala Amboli) Quantity
Turmeric powder
 1 Tsp
Red Chilli Powder 1 Tbsp
Ginger Garlic Paste 1 Tbsp
Chopped vegetables of choice 1-2

Let's Begin

  1. In a kneading bowl, add the above ingredients to flour & mix properly (except vegetables).
  2. Slowly add warm water as you stir continuously to make a thick enough, pouring consistency batter. 
  3. Remove any lumps by stiring, then add finely chopped vegetables (optional).
  4. Keep the batter aside for 20 min.
  5. Pour the batter on a non-stick pan and spread evenly. For normal pan, add little oil or butter to grease the pan before pouring the batter. 

That's it ! Soft and spongy Amboli is ready, serve with your favourite chutney
