Thank you for stopping by and viewing our products. Your support has helped us deliver better quality and authentic Maharashtrian cuisines at best rates !

This is the story behind Haveeka, I served for 13 years in my medical profession at different hospitals from Municipal corporation of Mumbai as Laboratory Technician till Feb 2007. Later, I worked for Intelenet Global Services till 2011.

In the year 2014, I started making different types of Bhajani flours on small scale which were well appreciated by my first customers at LIC of India, owing to which I launched Haveeka under my own company Pravid Food Products in July 2014. Initially, we catered to individual customers and later grew our business to supply products to food caterers, restaurants, retail shops and international customers.

From August 2019, Pravid Food Products entered into two new ventures - Dry Gravy Preparations, that are fresh and contain absolutely no preservatives or colors;  and Vaccum packaging of dry food items like thepla, paratha, puranpoli, plain phulka etc. that can be packaged for your students, relatives staying abroad. 

At Haveeka, we deliver best quality at best price !
